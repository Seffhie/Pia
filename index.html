About Us

Our Story

Welcome to Pagsuyo, home of the La Salle Filipiniana Dance Company, a prestigious dance organization with a 31-year legacy at De La Salle University - Dasmari単as. Our upcoming major event, 'PAGSUYO: Sayaw at Musika ng Pag-Ibig,' celebrates the rich courtship and marriage traditions from diverse Philippine regions. Join us for an unforgettable cultural experience that blends history, music, and movement in a mesmerizing showcase.

Production

Showcase Highlights

Courtship

Dance of Love

Immerse yourself in the enchanting courtship dances that depict the romantic rituals of Filipino culture. Each movement tells a story of love and tradition, capturing the essence of courtship in its most captivating form.

Genres

Unity in Dance

Experience the joy and symbolism of marriage through our dynamic choreography from Spanish Influence, Rural, Tribal, Cordillera, and Muslim Suites. Witness the union of hearts and souls as dancers portray the beauty and significance of love across the Philippines.

Traditions

Cultural Extravaganza

Discover the rich tapestry of Philippine culture through our traditional dance performances. From colorful costumes to lively music, our production showcases the diverse customs and heritage of each region, creating a vibrant celebration of Filipino identity.
